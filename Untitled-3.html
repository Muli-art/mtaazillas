WHO WE ARE

Rooted in Purpose. Forged in Community.

Mtaazillas is a grassroots youth movement founded on discipline, creativity, and collective action. Born in Nairobi, built by young people who refused to wait for change.

"We don't just talk about change — we are the change."

WHAT WE DO

We Build. We Lead. We Transform.

Three pillars that define every action we take as a movement.

I

Community Building

Creating strong internal networks and local support systems through consistent gatherings and shared labor.

II

Creative Expression

Empowering the youth to tell their own stories through art, digital media, and grassroots journalism.

III

Civic Action

Direct engagement with community leaders and stakeholders to ensure youth voices are central to policy.
